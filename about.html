About Dr. Alex

Dr. Alex Kisitu has been serving the community for over 15 years with dedicated eye care services. After graduating with honors from the prestigious University of Vision Sciences, Dr. Alex completed advanced training in specialized eye care procedures and treatments.

As a board-certified optometrist, Dr. Alex combines the latest technological advancements with a personalized approach to patient care. His commitment to continuing education ensures that his patients receive the most current and effective treatments available.

Dr. Alex Kisitu's philosophy is simple: every patient deserves individualized attention and the highest standard of care. This dedication to excellence has earned him numerous accolades and the trust of thousands of satisfied patients throughout his career.
